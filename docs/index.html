About Me

Background

My journey into the world of software development began during my graduate studies in Computer Science. While pursuing my Master’s degree, I had the opportunity to work as a Graduate Research Assistant, where I collaborated on real-world projects, honing my software engineering skills. This early exposure laid a strong foundation for me in understanding complex systems and problem-solving.

After completing my master's, I stepped into the professional world as a Full Stack Developer at the junior level, where I quickly grew by taking on increasingly complex challenges. I specialized in developing RESTful APIs using Java and Spring Boot, and I worked on building user-friendly web applications with Angular. My role also expanded into leadership as I was entrusted with mentoring junior developers, which strengthened my skills in communication, collaboration, and guiding others toward success.

I gained hands-on experience in implementing CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins, containerizing applications with Docker, and managing infrastructure through Terraform. This allowed me to deploy and scale applications efficiently, particularly when leveraging Kubernetes on AWS for cloud-native solutions. Working with Kafka introduced me to real-time data processing, which enhanced my ability to integrate data seamlessly into applications for better performance and reliability.

Throughout my journey, I’ve always thrived in Agile environments, focusing on maintaining code quality through unit and integration testing. What excites me most about technology is how it can drive meaningful change, and I’ve found that clear communication, initiative, and continuous learning are essential in building high-impact solutions.

With 3 years of experience as a Full Stack Developer, I’m passionate about crafting scalable, efficient, and secure applications while continuously evolving as a developer.
